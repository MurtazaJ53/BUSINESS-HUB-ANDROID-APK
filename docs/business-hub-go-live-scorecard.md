# Business Hub Go-Live Scorecard

## Purpose

This scorecard decides whether the curated Business Hub product is ready for controlled release.

## Product identity gate

- Business Hub is the visible product
- ERPNext remains hidden from normal clients
- client-facing wording is outcome-based
- navigation feels role-aware and plan-aware

Status:

- ready / not ready

## Role gate

- cashier flows complete
- manager flows complete
- owner flows complete
- support admin flows complete

Status:

- ready / not ready

## Plan gate

- Starter is lean and safe
- Growth unlocks operations cleanly
- Pro unlocks richer reporting and deeper controls cleanly
- upgrade path is visible for owner/admin roles

Status:

- ready / not ready

## Backend boundary gate

- curated API is the only client contract
- plan-tier redaction is enforced in backend summaries
- internal-only controls are not exposed to normal roles

Status:

- ready / not ready

## Support gate

- role-based training exists
- support playbook exists
- ERP escalation map exists
- issue ownership is clear

Status:

- ready / not ready

## Rollout gate

- pilot workspace prepared
- role accounts prepared
- acceptance scorecard completed
- known blockers documented

Status:

- ready / not ready

## Final decision

Choose one:

- release to pilot
- hold and fix
- release to limited plan/role set only

## Signoff

- product owner
- engineering owner
- operations owner
- support owner
